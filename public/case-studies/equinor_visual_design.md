# Equinor Visual Design Scheme

## 1. Design Philosophy

### Overall Visual Direction

A design that balances industrial strength with forward-thinking energy transition. The aesthetic is inspired by the clean, authoritative layouts of The Economist and the sophisticated, engineering-focused branding of Polestar. The concept is to visually articulate the dual narrative of a company rooted in traditional energy while boldly investing in the renewable technologies of tomorrow, using a structured, confident design language.

### Content Density

High-Medium Density. This presentation requires a balance between strategic analysis and visual storytelling. Each slide should convey 2-4 key points, often supported by data visualizations or impactful imagery of energy assets. The layout should maintain around 15-20% white space to ensure clarity and a professional, uncluttered feel.

## 2. Color System

**Primary Colors**

- Background color: #F5F5F3 (Soft off-white, creating a clean, premium canvas)
- Primary color: #0D2B45 (Deep ocean blue, representing offshore heritage and stability)
- Secondary color: #5A7D9A (Steel blue, suggesting industrial strength and engineering)
- Accent color: #E46A1F (Energetic orange, symbolizing innovation and energy transition)

**Text Colors**

- Dark text (for light backgrounds): #212121 (High-contrast black for optimal readability)
- Light text (for dark backgrounds): #FFFFFF (Pure white for text on dark sections)

## 3. Font Design

**Heading Fonts**

- English fonts
  - Quattrocento Sans (A classic, elegant sans-serif that conveys authority and trustworthiness, ideal for strategic headlines)
  - Oranienbaum (A high-contrast serif that adds a touch of classic, established elegance, perfect for impactful titles)

**Body Fonts**

- English fonts
  - Quattrocento Sans (Highly legible and clean, ensuring that complex financial and strategic data is easy to read)
  - Sorts Mill Goudy (A classic serif with excellent readability, lending a formal, credible tone to detailed analysis)

## 4. Image Style

High-quality, professional photography with a focus on scale and engineering. Feature dramatic shots of offshore oil platforms, wind farms at sea, and vast industrial landscapes. The style should be cinematic and authoritative, often using wide angles to convey magnitude. For abstract concepts, use macro shots of industrial materials or data visualization-inspired imagery. The overall mood is powerful, clean, and forward-looking.
