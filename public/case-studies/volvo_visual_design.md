# Volvo Visual Design Scheme

## 1. Design Philosophy

### Overall Visual Direction

A design that is both bold and refined, reflecting Volvo's commitment to safety, Scandinavian heritage, and modern innovation. The aesthetic draws inspiration from the clean, functional minimalism of Polestar and the confident, sophisticated typography of Volvo's own brand language. The core idea is to create a visual experience that feels as premium, safe, and forward-thinking as a Volvo vehicle itself.

### Content Density

High-Medium Density. This presentation balances impactful visuals with strategic analysis. Each slide should feature 2-4 key points, supported by data or diagrams, with a layout that allows for approximately 15%-20% white space to maintain clarity and a premium feel.

## 2. Color System

**Primary Colors**

- Background color: #F4F4F4 (Light grey, clean Scandinavian aesthetic)
- Primary color: #003057 (Volvo Ocean Blue, brand signature)
- Secondary color: #52565A (Volvo Dark Grey, sophisticated contrast)
- Accent color: #00A0DF (Volvo Electric Blue, innovation and technology)

**Text Colors**

- Dark text (for light backgrounds): #212121 (Almost black, high readability)
- Light text (for dark backgrounds): #FFFFFF (Pure white, maximum contrast)

## 3. Font Design

**Heading Fonts**

- English fonts
  - Hedvig Letters Sans (Distinctive and modern, its unique character reflects Volvo's innovative and human-centric brand personality)
  - Quattrocento Sans (Classic and elegant, providing a sophisticated and highly readable alternative that speaks to timeless quality)

**Body Fonts**

- English fonts
  - Quattrocento Sans (Clean, highly legible, and warm, ensuring maximum clarity for strategic information and data)
  - Sorts Mill Goudy (Classic and highly readable, its elegant form is perfect for longer text passages and detailed analysis)

## 4. Image Style

High-quality, professional photography with a cinematic feel. Focus on the sleek, sophisticated design of Volvo vehicles, often shot in minimalist, modern urban environments or expansive, clean Nordic landscapes. Images should emphasize safety, technology, and Scandinavian lifestyle. For non-product shots, use abstract close-ups of premium materials (leather, wood, metal) and clean architectural lines.
