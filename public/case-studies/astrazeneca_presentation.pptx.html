Company Overview

Science-Led Biopharmaceutical Leader

Corporate Foundation

AstraZeneca PLC is a global, science-led biopharmaceutical company focused on the discovery, development, and commercialization of prescription medicines. Founded in 1913 and headquartered in Cambridge, UK, the company has transformed into an innovation-driven pharmaceutical leader.

Under CEO Pascal Soriot, AstraZeneca has executed a remarkable turnaround, pivoting from traditional pharmaceuticals to science-led biopharmaceutical innovation with industry-leading R&D productivity and pipeline strength.

The company has established sustained global PARP inhibitor market leadership with Lynparza, while Enhertu has become the standard of care in HER2-positive and HER2-low metastatic breast cancer.

CVRM & R&I Excellence

Cardiovascular, Renal & Metabolism

CVRM is AstraZeneca's second-largest therapy area, led by Farxiga (dapagliflozin), the world's leading SGLT2 inhibitor with expanding indications across heart failure, chronic kidney disease, and type 2 diabetes.

Farxiga has demonstrated unprecedented cardiovascular and renal protection benefits beyond glycemic control, creating new treatment paradigms and significant market expansion opportunities.

Respiratory & Immunology

R&I represents AstraZeneca's biologics franchise for severe respiratory diseases, led by Fasenra for severe asthma and the rapidly growing Tezspire, which addresses a broader severe asthma population.

The segment benefits from biologic-driven treatment paradigms that transform care for patients with severe asthma and COPD, with significant unmet medical need.
