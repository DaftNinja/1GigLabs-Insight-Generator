Business Model & Strategic Vision

Edge Data Center Innovation

nLighten is a digital infrastructure platform developing innovative edge data centers distributed across European key economic hubs. Founded in 2021 by industry veterans from Equinix and Interxion, the company provides best-in-class network access to regional, national, and global enterprises.

Sustainability-First Approach

nLighten aligns edge data centers with the green transition through heat reuse partnerships, onsite renewable generation, and grid stabilization services, creating a virtuous cycle between increasing data consumption and environmental sustainability.
