Positioning in Global Data Center Markets

  • Direct Competitors: Digital Realty (16% market share), Iron Mountain (18%), American Tower (31%)
  • Hyperscale Cloud: AWS, Azure, Google Cloud (indirect competition)
  • Regional Players: NTT, QTS, CyrusOne, KDDI, China Telecom
  • Market Size: $100B+ global data center market

Competitive Advantages

  • Global Footprint: Unmatched metro coverage
  • Network Density: 499,000+ interconnections
  • Ecosystem: 10,000+ customers across multiple verticals
  • Platform: Software-defined interconnection capabilities
  • Sustainability: Leading ESG credentials

Strategic Differentiation

vs. Competitors: While competitors like Digital Realty focus on hyperscale and colocation, Equinix maintains leadership in interconnection and enterprise ecosystem. The retirement of Metal sharpens this focus on core differentiated capabilities.
