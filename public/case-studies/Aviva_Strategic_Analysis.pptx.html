Company Overview

Business Model & Strategic Focus

Market Leadership

Aviva is the UK's largest diversified insurer, operating across insurance, wealth, and retirement. The company serves 25+ million customers across UK, Ireland, and Canada, with leading market positions in life insurance (26% share), workplace pensions, and general insurance.

Strategic Transformation

Following a significant portfolio transformation, Aviva has simplified its business model to focus on core markets. The company is accelerating capital-light growth in wealth, health, and general insurance, targeting 75%+ capital-light business by 2028.
