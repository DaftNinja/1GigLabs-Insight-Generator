Company Overview

Sweden's Retail Leader

Leading Grocery Retail Group

ICA Gruppen is Sweden's largest and most successful grocery retail group, operating a unique cooperative model with nearly 1,300 stores across the country. The company has built its market leadership through a deep understanding of local markets and strong partnerships with independent retailers.

Mission: Making Every Day Easier

ICA Gruppen's strategic mission is to make every day a little easier for customers by offering personalised solutions, helping customers save time and energy, enabling sustainable choices, and delivering value for money across all touchpoints.

Market-Leading Grocery Operations

Unique Business Model

ICA Sweden operates a distinctive cooperative model where independent retailers own and operate their own stores while maintaining agreements with ICA Sweden for coordinated sourcing, logistics, marketing, and store development. This model combines local entrepreneurship with national scale advantages.
