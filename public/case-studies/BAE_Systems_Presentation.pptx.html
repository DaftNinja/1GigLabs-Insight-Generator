Company Overview

Leading Defense Contractor

BAE Systems is a British multinational defense, security, and aerospace company, ranked as one of the largest global defense corporations. The company provides some of the world's most advanced, technology-led defense solutions across air, land, sea, space, and cyber domains.

Global Presence

With operations in 40+ countries and a skilled workforce of approximately 109,700 employees, BAE Systems serves military, intelligence, and civilian branches of international governments. The company has strong market positions in the UK, US, Saudi Arabia, and Australia.

Heritage & Evolution

BAE Systems was established in 1999 through the merger of British Aerospace (BAe) with Marconi Electronic Systems (MES), creating one of the world's largest defense contractors.

The company's heritage traces back to the Royal Gunpowder Mills in Waltham Abbey, with origins spanning multiple renowned companies across various industries globally.

Today, BAE Systems is at the forefront of digital transformation in defense, integrating AI, autonomous systems, and advanced cybersecurity into its portfolio.
