# Lufthansa Strategic Analysis Visual Design Scheme

## 1. Design Philosophy

### Overall Visual Direction

This design embodies a premium, dynamic, and professional aesthetic, reflecting Lufthansa's position as Europe's leading airline with German engineering precision. Inspired by the clean layouts of *Monocle* magazine and the confident visual language of *BMW*, the design uses Lufthansa's signature colors and elegant typography to convey quality, reliability, and strategic intelligence.

### Content Density

High Density: This style is designed for data-rich business reports and strategic analysis. Each slide should efficiently present 3-5 key points or data visualizations, prioritizing clarity and information depth with a content-to-whitespace ratio of approximately 80/20.

## 2. Color System

**Primary Colors**

- Background color: #F8F9FA (Light neutral background, providing a clean canvas)
- Primary color: #003366 (Lufthansa Blue, conveying trust, reliability, and premium quality)
- Secondary color: #FFD700 (Lufthansa Yellow, representing energy, optimism, and German heritage)
- Accent color: #4A5568 (Slate gray, offering subtle differentiation and depth)

**Text Colors**

- Dark text (for light backgrounds): #212529 (Near-black for optimal readability)
- Light text (for dark backgrounds): #FFFFFF (Pure white for contrast)

## 3. Font Design

**Heading Fonts**

- English fonts
  - Oranienbaum (Modern serif with elegant, high-contrast letterforms, conveying premium quality and sophistication for main titles)
  - Quattrocento Sans (Clean, elegant sans-serif for subheadings, offering high readability and modern feel)

**Body Fonts**

- English fonts
  - Quattrocento Sans (Primary body text font, offering excellent readability and neutral, professional tone)
  - Sorts Mill Goudy (Classic serif body font, provides traditional, book-like reading experience for detailed text)

## 4. Image Style

Imagery should be premium and dynamic, favoring high-quality photography of modern aircraft, elegant airport environments, and sophisticated travel experiences. Focus on conveying German engineering precision and global connectivity. Data visualizations should be clean and minimalist, using the defined color palette to highlight key insights with a style similar to charts in *The Economist* or aviation industry publications.
