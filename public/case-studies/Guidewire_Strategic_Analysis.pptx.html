Business Model & Strategic Focus

Market Leadership

Guidewire is the worldwide leader in P&C insurance core systems, serving 300+ customers globally. The company provides InsuranceSuite—a comprehensive platform for policy administration, claims management, and billing that has become the industry standard for speed, agility, and success.

Platform Strategy

Guidewire Cloud, built on AWS, enables insurers to modernize core systems and turn IT into a strategic enabler. The platform combines policy, claims, and billing with advanced analytics, AI/ML capabilities, and an extensive marketplace of 200+ partner integrations.
