01 • Group Overview

320 Years of Serving Britain

Who We Are

Lloyds Banking Group is the UK's largest retail and commercial financial services provider, serving approximately 30 million customers through our iconic brands. With a heritage spanning over 320 years, we have been at the heart of British banking since 1695.

Our dual headquarters in London and Edinburgh reflect our commitment to serving communities across the entire United Kingdom. As a FTSE 100 constituent with a market capitalisation of approximately £59.9 billion, we combine the stability of a heritage institution with the innovation of a digital leader.
